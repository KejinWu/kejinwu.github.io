About me

Welcome! I am an Assistant Professor at Loyola University Chicago.

I was fortunate to complete my Ph.D. in Statistics at the University of California San Diego in 2025 under the supervision of Dimitris N. Politis.

My research interests lie in the area of Time Series Analysis, Bootstrap/Subsampling, and Machine Learning methods, especially for their applications in prediction. My CV is here.
